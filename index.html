ridn.

ridn. is a minimalist EPUB and MOBI reader for iPhone and iPad with a word-stream reading mode. It reads the books you choose, at the speed you choose.

Support

Questions, problems, or ideas — email ridn.reader@protonmail.com. Include your iOS version and, if a book misbehaves, its file format (EPUB or MOBI).

Found a bug? You can also report it publicly on the issue tracker.

Common questions

Where are my books stored? On your device, and nowhere else. ridn. has no accounts, no cloud, and no servers.

How do I read faster? Open the word stream (the mark at the top right of a book), tap play, and slide your finger up or down while reading to tune the tempo.

What does ridn. Pro unlock? Reading speeds above 250 words per minute, up to 1,200. One purchase, yours forever. Restore it any time from the same screen.
