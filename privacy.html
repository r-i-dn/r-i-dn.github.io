Privacy policy

ridn. — EPUB Speed Reader · Last updated: July 2026

ridn. collects no data. None.

Everything the app does happens on your device:

  • Books you open are read from files you select and stored only on your device.
  • Reading positions, speed settings, and preferences are stored only on your device.
  • There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no servers. The app makes no network requests with your data, because it has none to send.

Purchases (ridn. Pro) are processed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID. We never see your payment details. Apple's handling of that transaction is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

If you delete the app, everything it stored goes with it.
